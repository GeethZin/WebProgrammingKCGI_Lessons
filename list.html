Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are my favourite food. Here is a recipe for diliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medeum heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
